## Sales-Data-Analysis-Dashboard-2024
 Sales Data Analysis Dashboard 2024 — a fully-featured Excel workbook with 6 sheets:
 
# 📊 Dashboard
 — Executive summary with 4 KPI cards (Total Revenue, vs Target, Units Sold, Avg Order Value) plus a full monthly summary table with live formulas.
 
#📈 Monthly
— All 12 months with Revenue, Target, Units, Attainment %, and MoM Growth %, plus a clustered bar chart (Revenue vs Target) and a line chart (MoM growth trend).

#🗺️ Regional
— North/South/East/West breakdown with revenue share %, RANK formulas, a pie chart, and a units bar chart.

#📦 Products 
— 5 products with revenue, margin %, margin $, share %, and rank — with two comparison charts.

#🏅 Leaderboard 
— 8 sales reps sorted by revenue with medal icons 🥇🥈🥉, quota attainment, deals closed, and customer rating.

#📋 Raw Data
— 254 transaction rows with auto-filter and a green-yellow-red color scale on the Revenue column.

#Pandas Analysis (5 summaries)
Monthly revenue with MoM growth %
Regional breakdown with share %
Product performance with avg price
Sales rep leaderboard with rank

#Quarterly rollup
Excel Workbook (6 sheets)

Regional Analysis — table + pie chart
